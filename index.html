Some of the exciting and cool stuff that we have been working on

Leveraging machine learning for customer segmentation and targeted marketing

We have built out a NLP Model that can help to classify and find out what is the probability of a potential client buying a particular service from a retail bank. Banks can then do customer segmentation and targeted marketing thereby improving operational efficency and increasing customer conversion adding tremendous value to the marketing funnel.

This project was for a Hackathon which was sponsored by Axis Bank in collaboration with NASSCOMM and Venturesity. We were in top two teams out of the extremely talented competing applicants from all over the country.

Connected machines using IoT pioneering Industry 4.0

In small manufacturing industries the OEE Calculation for CNC/VMC is done manually. Machine utilization parameters are manually measured by factory workers. This kind of approach is inefficient, tedious and time consuming.

We are working with manufacturing companies who are major automobile component suppliers to automobile giants to build an integrated system that connects to machine, extract data in an automated manner and relay it to cloud.

This application is aimed at providing seamless integration of entire enterprise providing better visibility to all key stakeholders with better predictability, reducing production down time and improving the OEE.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Looks like FRISSON GLOBAL does pretty much everything in Technology, what is niche or specific area of expertise of the company?

    Internet of things and connected world domain is complex in terms of the different components and systems involved. Our main aim is to help maximize value creation (by investing in Technology)for the organizations that we work with. Often this involves working with multiple systems and their integration. We are Technologists at heart and Frugal Innovation is our niche and we are focussed on reducing the turn around time and maximizing the value of deliverables.

  • Do you have subject matter expertise in all the technology verticals that you operate in?

    Technology is evolving at a very rapid pace. We have expertise to the concepts needed for delivery of a project and the hands-on learning framework that we have built helps the team to learn any new concept and deliver within weeks.We have robust understanding of technology fundamentals and technology platforms and expertise on how systems operate in tandem with each other. Our expertise is in system integration to build solutions that not only solve a particular problem, but also have a clear visible impact in terms of value creation. We have a strong team that is upto edge with latest technological developments and pretty nimble to learn any new anything and start to deliver. We collaborate with 3rd party Subject Matter Experts as and when needed.

  • What about domain expertise?

    Innovation is all about breaking silos and collaboration. We hire best in class industry consultants who have had deep dive domain expertise on a project to project basis. Quite often the organizations that approach us have the expertise in house but we collaborate with experts from industry. Strategically we are looking to build a team of subject matter experts. We are always looking for great talent, talk to our talent development team to see areas and opportunites you can explore.

  • How are you different from traditional technology consulting and services based organizations?

    Unlike traditional technology consulting firms, where the solution to a problem is generally obvious and often involes setting up an ERP or developing and deploying an application on a technology platform and setting up the infrastructure, we work on problems where the solution is not generally very obvious and out of the box thinking is required.

  • Why and how are you so different from companies that do work on innovative solutions?

    We are working on developing an internal proprietary framework for innovative problem solving. The unique thing about this framework is that it takes into consideration multiple input parameters to give a clear picture of the problem at hand. Based on this evaluation model we suggest to you whether the investment in technology will be of value add. Post this we calculate the return on investment and the time to recovery of your capital. Once everything is done we put a feasibility assesment report that proposes the possible implementations available. The intent is to maximize the value creation process in a structured manner.

  • Is research and development your forte? What differences do you see between research and development and innovation?

    No, we are not a pure research and development based organization. Although the solutions that we develop sometimes involve deep diving into details but this is not what we are focus is. Our focus is to build solutions using the existing available technologies and platforms and create a meaningful interaction between them to maximize the value and impact of the deliverable. In a broad sense,research and development often involves inventing something new or doing something in a radically better manner by modelling the cause and effect relationship, the return on investment is undeterministic and the time to get the return on capital is sometimes very high. Innovation to us is all about applying concepts leveraging existing technologies and platforms to create products or solutions that create substantial value.

  • Looks like you are really obsessed with return on investment of the capital, are solutions you work on only focussed on improving returns?

    With profit making organizations they want to see clear impact of a technology adoption in their business. Investment in technology has to justify significant returns. That is what our focus is. In some cases the returns might not be immediate but more strategic in terms of process or quality improvement. With citizen science projects the returns are intangible with benefits that it has to offer to the environment for years, these are kind of projects usually sponsored by Municipal Corporations and Governments.

  • Your website domain says ".org", are you a Non Profit Organization?

    No, we are not a Non Profit Organization. This was the domain name available at the time of registration that is the only reason. Domain names are just placeholder to reach to the point. We are trying to bring thought leadership and a paradigm shift in the industry with how we do things in a different manner.
